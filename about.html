About Us

Al Yarmouk steel & Engineering Co. LLC  was established in 1982 in Ajman, UAE as a result of the founder's vision that the region will be a great marketplace for steel buildings in the near future. To satisfy the needs for providing engineering services in steel structures, a company manned by professionals was therefore established. In 1995, the company opened it's sister concern in Dubai naming it Al Yarmouk Metal Construction LLC.

WHO WE ARE

We deploy the latest methodologies in metal fabrication and steel structure erection

The company was established to supply state-of-the-art fabricated structural steel in the industry. We are equipped with the latest design and detailing software to ensure the accuracy of our engineering staff's output in addition to the expertise of our current employees. Our plant has all the machineries and equipments needed to manufacture PEB steel structures and to fabricate hot rolled steel structures in the shortest possible time. Additionally, our highly skilled site installation team and hi-tech accessibility equipments mean we can plan and complete structures erection and installation in the most efficient manner possible.
